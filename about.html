About Us






The Balloonatic Asylum was formed in 2012 by Krista H. Elium. The Balloonatic Asylum sells high quality latex and mylar balloons at affordable prices. We make beautiful hand-crafted balloons for any occasion, recreating ideas at the customers request. All you need to do is send us a photo of what you want, and we'll bring it to life as a quality balloon adding that personal touch. We pride ourselves on listening to our customers.



Mission Statement

To become the leader in the balloon event and party supply industry by offering the best products and services at competitive prices across all facets within a multi-channel platform of corporates, retail and on-line business, to be accessible, convenient, affordable and professional in all services. We offer an enjoyable customer service experience and deliver superior quality.
